HOW TO DUEL

  1. 👆Tap a stance on the wheel each round — you both reveal at once.
  2. ↻You beat the 2 stances clockwise of yours: CLEAN −2, then GLANCE −1.
  3. ⇄Directly across = WHIFF — blades pass, no damage.
  4. ⚔Same stance = CLASH → break the bind: Drive ▸ Slip ▸ Trap ▸ Drive.
